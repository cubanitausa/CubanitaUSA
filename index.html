About Mailyn

Cuban-born. Bilingual. Built for the work of translating hard ideas for real audiences.

Mailyn Salabarria is a bilingual communications strategist, public speaker, and media host with more than 15 years of experience across advocacy, journalism, outreach, and public relations. Cuban-born and a survivor of communism, she brings lived experience to conversations about freedom, markets, and civic life across the Americas.
