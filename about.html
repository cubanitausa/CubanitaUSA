About

Mailyn Salabarria

Bilingual communications strategist, public speaker, media host, and consultant with experience across advocacy, media, outreach, public relations, and international audiences.

Story

Cuban-born. Bilingual. Bicultural.

Mailyn was born in Cuba and lived under communism before becoming a U.S. citizen in 2001. That lived experience — not theory, not talking points — shapes how she thinks and speaks about freedom, markets, and civic life across the Americas today.

She holds a JD from the University of Havana and an MS in Journalism & Mass Communication, with a bilingual investigative focus, from Florida International University. Her career spans investigative journalism, bilingual outreach, donor relations, event planning, and political messaging consulting — more than 15 years of strategic communications work across the U.S. and Latin America.
